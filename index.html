What is the Liturgical Year

The liturgical calendar is a calendar composed of seasons such as lent, easter, advent, ordinary time and christmas. Each season has its own color, prayers, dates and meaning. It is also called church year

Why do we Celebrate the Liturgical Year

While the calendar seems the same as a regular calendar, we celebrate the liturgical Year by following the liturgical calendar and because we value the life of Christ.
